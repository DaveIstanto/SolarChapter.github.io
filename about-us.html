Meet Our Team

Some people are born into families where they have to walk for hours just to get water, all we have to do is turn on the faucet. We are Solar Chapter, a non-profit organization consisting of students and alumni from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. We want to help people who are less fortunate because we believe everyone deserves a shot at a better life. The world could always use more love. Join us in improving their living condition, education, and opportunity!
