Chapter 1

Water Pump for Umutnana

East Nusa Tenggara, Indonesia

More than 700 people in Umutnana Village, East Nusa Tenggara, lack access to clean water. They have to walk two kilometers from their houses for access to the water source. Each day they need to make at least three trips which is equals to at least four hours of their precious time, the time they could have spent to do something more productive rather than to get life’s basic need.

Our Solution

Solar Chapter will collaborate with PAMSIMAS (A National Platform for Rural Water Supply and Sanitation) to provide easier access of clean water. We plan to build a reservoir to collect water from three water sources, which will then deliver the water to the distribution tank near the village by using solar pumps. The distance between the reservoir and the distribution tank is around 600 meters. Your generous donation would allow Solar Chapter to accommodate 324 people and PAMSIMAS will accommodate the remaining 300 residents in Umutnana. PAMSIMAS will also build a piping system to distribute water from distribution tank to 16 locations across the village.

“It was never enough water for their homes, and it left them little time to earn money, study, and achieve their dreams.”
